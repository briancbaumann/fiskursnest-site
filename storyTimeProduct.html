Fiskur's Story Time

Create magical, narrated adventures your child will love — again and again!

With just a few taps, kids can build their own custom stories by picking favorite characters, choosing silly items like bubble blasters or banana phones, and setting the stage for fun in magical forests, outer space, or even on a submarine.

Once the story is ready, it’s brought to life with expressive voice narration. Kids can choose from a variety of narrator voices in the app settings to match their mood or favorite style. Whether your child wants to read along, listen quietly, or imagine their own twists, every story becomes a unique experience.

  • Create stories in seconds with customizable characters, settings, and props
  • Narrated playback with voice options kids can choose
  • Replay favorite stories from each character’s story library
  • Encourages imagination and storytelling skills
  • Safe, ad-free environment made just for kids
